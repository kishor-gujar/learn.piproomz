What is MetaTrader 4?

Since all Forex trading is done through the internet, you will need trading software. MetaTrader4 is one the most powerful and popular Forex trading clients in the world, supported by a large percentage of Forex brokers. MetaTrader 4, or MT4 for short, supports clients for Windows, MacOS, Android and iPhone/iPad/ Android.

What are Signals?

Signals are a feature of MT4 that facilitates the copying of trading operations from one account to another. There are many signals featured on the MT4 platform, both free and paid. You can see on the Signals tab of the MT4 interface that signal providers are displayed and sorted in the order of their trading results. Detailed statistics, metrics and graphs are provided for each one. Subscribing to a signal allows that signal to trigger executions on your own account. Some brokers offer signals and some do not. Make sure to check with your broker before you commit any funds if you want to use signals.
