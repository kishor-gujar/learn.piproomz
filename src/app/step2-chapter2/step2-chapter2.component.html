How Much Does Forex Trading Cost?

Forex trading can be undertaken without a lot of capital. Minimum deposits that brokers allow clients to open an account with can be as little as $100 USD. This does not mean it’s wise to deposit a very small amount of money. It is always best to deposit as much as you feel comfortable with. This will give you more trading power and more money to utilize in managing your trades. Pip Roomz suggests to fund the broker with a minimun of 500-1k USD.
